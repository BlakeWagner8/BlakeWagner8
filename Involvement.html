Greek Life

Blake is a member of the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity. He rushed in the spring of 2021 and is on the presidents committee as well as assisting with philanthropy events. He plans on taking a leadership role within the organization at some point in his college career ATO is the leadership development fraternity founded on September 11, 1865 at Virginia Military Institute by Otis Allan Glazebrook, Alfred Marshall, and Erskine Mayo Ross. It was founded based on Christian principles with a strong focus on brotherhood in order to unite the north and the south after the Civil War.

Leadership Mentors

Leadership Mentors is a leadership organization at NKU that focuses on helping students to be better leaders while also building the leadership skills of its own members. Blake became a member of Leadership Mentors in the spring of 2021 and hopes to get more involved with the organization in the future, especially after coronavirus.
