Bio

Blake is a freshman at Northern Kentucky University majoring in Cybersecurity with a Minor in Business. He is originally from Florence, Kentucky, about 30 minutes away from NKU. In 2020, he graduated from Dixie Heights High School. Throughout high school, Blake played basketball and was involved in volunteering with clubs such as National Honor Society, Beta Club, and Scholars Club while also working a job. In college he joined the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity and is also a member of Leadership Mentors and the Cyber Defense Team. A few of Blake's hobbies include watching and playing sports, traveling, playing video games, listening to music, and playing guitar.
